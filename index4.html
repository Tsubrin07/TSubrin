Hobbies

Listening to Music

Music is an integral part of my life, and I absolutely love listening to music! to be precise,piano music. It helps to calm and de-stress my mind and increases concentration. Apart from that, I also admire rock music, it makes me feel energised and happy. Although I appreciate music of all genre, but these are the ones that I mostly listen to.



Reading Books

Ever since I was a child, I was always enthusiastic about reading story books and novels. Drawn to the stories that unfold through these pages, the mystery, the anticipation, the tremendous joy I receive from reading is indescribable. gave me tremendous joy. It feels as if you're consumed into a whole new world that is being described in the books. Some of my favourite authors include Paulo Coelho, Khaled Hosseini, Sidney Sheldon and Suzanne Collins.



Making DIY crafts

The first time I discovered my interest in creating something artistic by myself was when my aunt gifted me a diy ornaments kit. It contained vibrant plastic beads of different shapes and sizes, elastic and inelastic strings; all that is required to design and create your own jewellery. I was extremely excited and tried making various things out of that amazing kit and even gifted those handmade jewelleries to my friends. During festivals, I decorated handmade cards and distributed them amongst all my near and dear ones.Also, I made origami butterflies and stars out of colourful papers and stick them to my wall. I used to spend hours watching youtube and learning new decorations from scraps everyday.



Baking

This is one of my most favourite hobbies of all time. Being a person who has a sweet tooth; I quickly picked up my interest in baking various delicacies whenever I craved for them. I also watch a lot of reality shows on cooking competition and follow a lot of famous pastry chefs on social media in order to gain motivation and be bewildered by their stunning bakeries. During my spare time, I try to pursue my hobby in baking as much as I can, and I absolutely love it!



Graphic Design

This is a hobby that I've recently started investing my time in. I was inspired to learn about graphic design from my brother, who shares the same interest as me in this case.In order to hone and pursue my skill I learned to use softwares such as Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop. I'm still an amature and have a long way to go to be fully efficient in this field.
